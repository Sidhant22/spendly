Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics providers that track individuals, or data brokers.

If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify you before any such integration takes effect.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
